Help & Support

Welcome to the Help page! Here you will find answers to frequently asked questions and guidance on how to use the TestMonitor platform effectively.

How to Get Started

  1. Sign Up: Create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" button and entering your details.
  2. Login: After signing up, you can login with your credentials.
  3. Take the Exam: Once logged in, you can select your desired exam and start it.
  4. KeyPress Monitoring: During the exam, your key presses will be monitored for security and integrity.
  5. Review Results: After completing the exam, you can view your results in your dashboard.
